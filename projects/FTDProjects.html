FTD Projects Overview

This section consolidates all FTD-related analyses, including seasonal sales trends, pricing strategies, and overall performance evaluations.

Valentine’s Day Sales Analysis

Analysis of seasonal sales trends, including top-selling flower varieties and color combinations for Valentine’s Day.

  • Hot pink, red, and lavender were among the top colors driving sales.
  • Roses, lilies, and tulips were the most popular flower types.
  • Seasonal promotions significantly impacted sales volume.

Mother’s Day Sales Analysis

Insights into Mother’s Day sales trends, including customer preferences for flower arrangements.

  • Unique color palettes such as peach and ivory were highly favored.
  • Analysis of sales performance by product sets showed the importance of diverse bouquet options.

Overall FTD Performance Analysis

Comprehensive review of FTD’s sales performance across multiple seasons, focusing on optimizing pricing and inventory management.

  • Correlation studies identified key drivers behind order values and volumes.
