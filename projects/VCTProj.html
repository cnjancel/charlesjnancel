Overview

This project analyzes data from the 2023 Valorant Champions Tour, focusing on key performance metrics of players and teams. The study examines factors such as win rates, individual player impact, team dynamics, and strategic elements that influenced match outcomes.

Results

The analysis identifies top-performing players, highlights strategic plays, and reveals patterns that contribute to winning strategies. Key findings include the importance of agent selection, map control, and round economy management.

Discussion

The insights from this analysis provide valuable perspectives for players, coaches, and analysts in the eSports community. Understanding the nuances of agent roles, team coordination, and individual performance metrics can guide strategic decision-making in future competitions.
