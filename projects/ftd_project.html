Overview

The FTD Profitability Analysis page provides insights into various projects aimed at enhancing FTD’s financial performance. Key analyses include evaluating current and new SRPs (Suggested Retail Prices), adjusting pricing formulas, and optimizing cost structures for Sympathy and EDay categories.

Key Projects

  • FTD Profitability Analysis: An in-depth review of current profitability metrics, focusing on cost adjustments and SRP recalibration.
  • Sympathy and EDay Pricing Adjustments: Comparative analysis of current vs. new SRPs for key categories, including visualizations that highlight the impact of pricing changes.
  • Formula Implementation: Applying new pricing formulas based on the 75th percentile margin to ensure competitive and profitable pricing strategies.
